WAYPOINT INTEL

Sector 1: Lake Geneva to Col de l'Iseran

Initial insertion followed by a rapid climb into the high mountains. Focus on fuel management and tire heat.

Sector 2: Val d'Isère to Briançon

Technical hairpins and high-exposure cliffs. Maintain visual scanning for mountain fauna and gravel patches.

Sector 3: Briançon to Nice

The final descent through the Gorges du Cians and the Verdon fringe. Temperature will rise significantly.
