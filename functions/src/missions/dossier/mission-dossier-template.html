SECTOR INTELLIGENCE

Phase 1: Alpine Entry & Initial Climb

Geneva to Col de l'Iseran

Riding Character: Sweeping valley roads transitioning into tight high-altitude switchbacks. Expect significant temperature drops as you ascend into the high technical sectors.

Logistics Note: Primary fuel stop at Val d'Isère. Gastronomy anchor: L'Atelier d'Edmond (2-Star Michelin) recommended for overnight recovery.

Phase 2: High Pass Technical Core

Val d'Isère to Briançon

Riding Character: Sustained technical concentration. The core of the mission. Col du Galibier requires precise throttle control and absolute focus.

Logistics Note: Fatigue management is critical here. Recovery stop at Briançon old town. POI: Cité Vauban (UNESCO World Heritage).

Phase 3: Mediterranean Descent

Briançon to Nice Coastal Arrival

Riding Character: Transitions from jagged alpine peaks to the softer, warmer air of the Mercantour. Technical descent towards the coastal extraction hub in Nice.

Logistics Note: Final exit at Nice Airport hub. Overnight anchor: Hotel Negresco for post-mission debrief and recovery.
